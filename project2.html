Water Stewardship

We manage our water resources effectively. A total of 23 check-dams have been installed around the community in which we operate. We have also carried out 6 ponds restoration works. All our manufacturing locations have world-class Effluent treatment and Sewage treatment plants. At ARE&M, we have Zero Discharge plants leading to an increase in effluent recycling rate and reduction in fresh-water consumption. Water meters are installed at all strategic inlets and outlets to accurately measure water consumption and treatment across all businesses.
